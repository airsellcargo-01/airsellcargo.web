# Airsell Cargo: Cargo Acceptance Checklist

Use this checklist for every shipment to ensure it meets carrier standards (e.g., Ethiopian Airlines) before it leaves Hargeisa.

## 📦 1. Physical Inspection
* **Packaging Integrity:** Is the packaging sturdy and free of damage?
* **Labeling:** Are destination labels clearly visible and securely attached?
* **Dimensions:** Have the Length, Width, and Height been measured accurately?
* **Actual Weight:** Has the cargo been weighed on a calibrated scale?

## 📄 2. Documentation Check
* **Air Waybill (AWB):** Is the AWB completed according to the SOP?
* **Commercial Invoice:** Does the description of goods match the AWB?
* **Packing List:** Is there a detailed list for multi-piece shipments?

## 🛡️ 3. Security & Compliance
* **Contents Verified:** Are the goods restricted, dangerous, or general cargo?
* **Security Screening:** Has the cargo passed local security requirements?
* **IATA Standards:** Does the shipment comply with non-IATA forwarder guidelines?
