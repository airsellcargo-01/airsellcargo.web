# Airsell Cargo - Hargeisa Branch

Welcome to the official digital repository for **Airsell Cargo**, a professional non-IATA Freight Forwarder based in Hargeisa.

## 🚢 Core Business Operations
* **Air Freight Logistics:** Managing cargo shipments, documentation, and local sales agency (CSA) coordination.
* **Cargo Management Systems:** Utilizing digital tools for efficient booking and shipment tracking.
* **Staff Training & Compliance:** Maintaining standards in Air Waybill (AWB) completion and basic cargo skills.

## 🛠 Active Operational Projects
* **Microsoft 365 Integration:** Migrating our business communication and domain (airsellcargo.com) to a cloud-based environment.
* **Direct Account Setup:** Establishing credit and cash accounts with major carriers to streamline shipping for our clients.
