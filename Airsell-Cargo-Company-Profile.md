# Company Profile: Airsell Cargo Service Agent

## 1. Executive Summary
Airsell Cargo is a professional freight forwarding agency based in Hargeisa, Somaliland. As a non-IATA forwarder, we specialize in providing streamlined air freight logistics, cargo handling, and supply chain solutions tailored to the regional market.

## 2. Business Information
* **Company Name:** Airsell Cargo Service Agent
* **Headquarters:** Hargeisa, Somaliland
* **Primary Domain:** airsellcargo.com
* **Branch Administrator:** Hamse Dahir
* **Core Sector:** International Freight Forwarding & Logistics

## 3. Mission & Vision
* **Mission:** To bridge the gap between global carriers and local businesses through efficient, transparent, and digitally-driven cargo management.
* **Vision:** To become the leading digital-first freight forwarder in the Horn of Africa, recognized for operational excellence and integrity.

## 4. Core Services
* **Air Freight Logistics:** Expert handling of international imports and exports via HGA.
* **Cargo Management:** Specialized tracking and documentation services for commercial clients.
* **Local Sales Agency (CSA):** Acting as a reliable local partner for international carriers.
* **Documentation & Compliance:** Specialized in Air Waybill (AWB) completion and customs-ready paperwork.

## 5. Digital Infrastructure
Airsell Cargo is committed to modernizing logistics through:
* **Cloud Operations:** Fully integrated Microsoft 365 environment for secure business communication.
* **Digital Tracking:** Future-ready integration with Transportation Management Systems (TMS).
* **Staff Excellence:** Continuous enrollment in carrier-mandated training (Basic Cargo Skills & AWB Rating).

## 6. Strategic Partners
We maintain professional relationships with major regional stakeholders, including:
* Ethiopian Airlines (Cargo Division)
* Local Cargo Sales Agents (CSA) in the Hargeisa region
* International digital booking platforms (cargo.one)
