Who We Are

Established in 2024, Airsell Cargo is a dedicated freight forwarding service agent based in Hargeisa. We specialize in bridging the gap between local businesses and global markets, providing efficient logistics management for air and sea freight.

Our Mission

As a specialized non-IATA agent, our mission is to modernize the logistics landscape in the region through transparency, digital integration, and reliable service. We focus on providing customized solutions for cargo moving through Hargeisa (HGA) and Berbera port.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Local Expertise: Deep understanding of the Hargeisa and Berbera logistics corridors.
  • ✅ Global Reach: Strong connections to Middle Eastern hubs and international carriers.
  • ✅ Digital First: We utilize modern tracking and documentation systems to keep your cargo safe.
